About Lani

I live in picturesque Clarens, South Africa. I have two degrees: B.Prim.Ed and BA Psych(Honns).
I used to be a teacher, but that's not my passion.
In 2008 I was in an accident which took the lives of both my sisters and also my ability to lift my left arm.
In the years since, I often thought about changing careers to do something I really love. I really love technology. I really love solving problems.
Now I can't imagine doing anything other than writing code, all day, every day.

Little over a year ago, I knew nothing about web development.
I was lucky enough to have some time.
I took care of my landlord's kids and their afternoon activities and homework, so I also had some money.
I have spent 12-18 hours a day for 13 months learning and building and working towards becoming a self taught developer.
It has taken a lot of dedication, but it's been so much fun!

HTML5, CSS3 and JavaScript is where I started. Along the way I also learned AJAX and JSON. I made sure that I had a solid grasp on vanilla JS, before I also learned jQuery and AngularJS.
I wanted to make web apps. So, I learned Meteor.js, because it is full-stack.
Next, I wanted to know about the backend. I started learning Python, and loved it.
I tried a little Django, but then I found and fell in love with Flask. I also learned MongoDB and a little bit of MySQL. Please visit my Projects page.

More learning! Everyday I learn something new. There is so much I still want to know, and be able to do.
Also, I've taken so much from open source resources that it's only right to give some back. That is what I'd like to do through my blog and contributing to open source projects.
Then I'd also like a j.o.b. please!
If you would like to work with me, please see my Contact page or find me on social media.
